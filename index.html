Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect at Dive Barn: Part 2?

This year's exhibition builds on the success of our inaugural event, featuring an expanded collection of immersive installations, local artists, and unique experiences set in our beautiful Mendocino barn venue.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have designated parking areas near the barn. Given the rural location, we recommend carpooling when possible and wearing comfortable shoes suitable for outdoor terrain.

What should I bring?

Bring yourself and an open mind! We recommend comfortable clothing and shoes. The barn can get cool in the evening, so consider bringing a light jacket.

Is food and drink available?

We'll have local refreshments available for purchase, featuring Mendocino's finest local vendors and artisanal offerings.

Can I take photos?

Photography is encouraged! We love seeing our event through your eyes. Please be respectful of other visitors and the artwork. Tag us @divebarn for a chance to be featured!
