Why We Share Everything

We believe in complete transparency about how Dive Barn is funded and where every dollar goes. By openly sharing our budget, expenses, and community contributions, we build trust with our supporters and demonstrate our commitment to responsible stewardship of the resources entrusted to us.

This page shows exactly what it costs to create an unforgettable weekend of music, art, and community connection. We're accountable to everyone who believes in this vision and chooses to support it.
