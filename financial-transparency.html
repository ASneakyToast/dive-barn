Why We Share Everything

The organizers don't take any money (and don't anticipate to). We're incredibly grateful that the Yorkville Schoolhouse Ranch space is given to us for free, but we're still responsible for minor renovations and improvements to keep it safe and beautiful.

Your support helps us pay for food and drink, tip Scott and his bartender (please tip your bartender!), and most importantly, pay our artists and bands meaningful stipends for all their incredible work and materials. All merchandise sales go directly to Quality Time, our design agency, who's giving us a subsidized rate for their amazing work.
